Bracelet to Regulate Electromagnetic Field of the Body

I created a design of a wearable bracelet to regulate electromagnetic field of the human body, one of the many energies that allow our body to function. I chose to design this product because of the increase in accessibility and familiarity of wearable technology in the market. The purpose is to regulate the electromagnetic field of the human body and promote energetic balanced and better health. Overall this device provides a solution to traditional monitoring approach to regulate the body and gives more autonomy and information to the user, but it is using fundamental principles from alternative medicine. Binkley, P. F. (2003). Predicting the potential of wearable technology. IEEE engineering in medicine and biology magazine, 22(3), 23-27.

User Personas

After deciding on an object to design, I developed several potential users. The main industries are for users who are open to alternative medicine, people who keeping track of their health using wearable technology in general, and potentially senior citizens who are more prone to get sick. From the larger range of potential users I narrowed the scope to Lucy, one persona who covers a large populations, but it is also narrow enough to design for. The reason I chose Lucy is because she has the desire to get real time feedback about her health, and is generally relatable to most americans because she has a 9-5 job, children, a lot of responsibilities and very limited free time.

Human Centered Design

I applied user centered design to the device by ensuring it is comfortable and aesthetically pleasing to the point people want to wear it. Similarly, I took into account the different activities people engage in, for example working in an office, outdoors, or being active at the gym. Furthermore, one of the largest challenges is facilitating a conceptual model for users who are not familiar with the electromagnetic field of the body and how it can help their health.

User Feedback and Testing

\

Given the feedback of the testing phase, the original design was altered to include a vibration sensation (depicted in the image by the zig-zag lines). In the new design, when there is an irregularity in electromagnetic field the light changing color to red and there is a vibration. The vibration of the bracelet allows for a more clear signifier of a change. The screen display of the region of the body which was affected was taken away to create more privacy for the user.
